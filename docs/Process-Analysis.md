# Process Analysis

## As-Is Process (Current State)

1. Patient travels to healthcare facility.
2. Appointment scheduled manually.
3. Long wait times.
4. Limited access to patient history.
5. Delayed diagnosis and treatment.

Pain Points:
- Travel costs
- Delayed intervention
- Service bottlenecks
- Inconsistent record management

---

## To-Be Process (Future State)

1. Patient logs into digital portal.
2. Books telehealth consultation.
3. Completes AI-assisted triage.
4. Doctor reviews patient history before consultation.
5. Virtual consultation conducted.
6. Follow-up scheduled digitally.

Improvements:
- Reduced travel requirements
- Faster appointment scheduling
- Better continuity of care
- Scalable and digital workflow
