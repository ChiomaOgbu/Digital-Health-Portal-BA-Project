# Business Case

## Executive Summary

This project proposes a scalable digital health portal designed to improve healthcare access in remote and rural communities. The solution leverages telehealth capabilities, AI-assisted triage, and offline functionality to address infrastructure and accessibility challenges.

## Strategic Alignment

The initiative aligns with:

- Digital transformation in healthcare
- Public health accessibility goals
- Cost-efficient service delivery
- Remote workforce enablement

## Expected Business Benefits

- Improved healthcare accessibility
- Reduced service delivery gaps
- Lower operational overhead through virtual consultations
- Faster clinical decision-making
- Scalable regional deployment

## Cost Considerations (Conceptual)

- Platform development & integration
- Infrastructure & cloud hosting
- Security & compliance implementation
- Training & onboarding

## Risk Assessment (High-Level)

- Connectivity limitations in rural regions
- Data privacy compliance requirements
- Adoption resistance from users
- Integration complexity with legacy systems

## Success Metrics

- Reduction in appointment wait time
- Increase in remote consultation uptake
- Improved patient satisfaction scores
- System uptime and performance targets
