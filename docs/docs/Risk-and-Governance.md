# Risk & Governance Framework

## Key Risks

| Risk | Impact | Mitigation |
|------|--------|------------|
| Data privacy breach | High | Encryption & access controls |
| Low user adoption | Medium | Training & user onboarding |
| Connectivity failure | High | Offline functionality |
| System downtime | High | Redundant hosting infrastructure |

---

## Governance Considerations

- Clear ownership model
- Defined escalation paths
- Regular stakeholder reporting
- Compliance with healthcare regulations
- Continuous performance monitoring
